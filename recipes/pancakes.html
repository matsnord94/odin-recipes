Pancakes

Sveriges godaste pannkakor

De bästa du kan göra med dina fiendes tårar

Ingredients

  • 500g of flour
  • 5 teapsoons of salt
  • 2 drops of enemys tears
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  1. Mix flower and eggs untill dry
  2. Add tears from your enemys
  3. Add salt
  4. Let it rest for 5 hours untill its ready to harvest.
